People

Faculty

Haining Wang

Prof. Wang joined UD ECE in fall 2014 as part of the new cybersecurity research group. His research interests lie in the areas of security, networking systems, cloud computing, mobile computing, and cyber-physical systems. The main thrusts of his current and past research include energy/power attacks and countermeasures inside data centers, covert channel attacks and countermeasures in the cloud, behavioral biometric-based user authentication, automatic online bot detection, transparent anti-phishing and countering distributed denial-of-service attacks.
